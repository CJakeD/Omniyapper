Inside (Chevy, HoyoMix)


INSIDE is the ninth original soundtrack album to be released for Honkai: Star Rail. It features a total of six soundtracks produced by the HOYO-MiX team, with Chevy on vocals as Robin's singing voice.

  • Sway to my Beat in the Cosmos
  • If I Can Stop One Heart From Breaking
  • Hope Is the Thing With Feathers
  • Had I Not Seen the Sun
