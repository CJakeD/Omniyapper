rapunzel (dhruv)


rapunzel is the debut EP by the singer/songwriter dhruv. The EP was released on January 21, 2022 through Little Worry/RCA Records. The EP’s first single double take was released on May 24, 2019. The song slowly gained traction on TikTok in 2021 with an official music video coming out later that year.
